The 10-Minute Million

Let’s face it - Raising money is difficult. And for most, it’s not a pleasant experience either. The complications of the tedious process, administrative formalities and non-standardized documents make it particularly irksome for first-timers. We are trying to revolutionize this process by taking an end-to-end care of your transaction, empowering you to raise INR 1 Million in just 10 Minutes!

E-Cell, IIT Bombay

The Entrepreneurship Cell is a non-profit organization run by the students of IIT Bombay that aims at manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of young students. We at E-Cell host various workshops, speaker sessions, innovative games and competitions for aspiring entrepreneurs all throughout the year and support them by providing necessary resources such as mentorship, consultancy, networking and angel support.

SINE, IIT Bombay

SINE, Society for Innovation and Entrepreneurship, hosted by Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay is an umbrella organization for promotion of entrepreneurship at IIT Bombay. SINE administers a business incubator which provides support for technology based entrepreneurship. Thus, SINE extends the role of IIT Bombay by facilitating the conversion of research activity into entrepreneurial ventures.

TermSheet.io

TermSheet is an end-to-end fund-raising solution for investors and entrepreneurs to close seed round investments rapidly. Inspired by the Series Seed documents prepared by Fenwick and West along with Marc Andreesen and open sourced in 2010 for deals in the US, Termsheet provides an India-centric solution and extends the concept by allowing parties to manage the deal- closing process in a hassle free manner.
